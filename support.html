We accept design support assignments from organizations to help them deeply embed design in their organizational culture, by integrating design not only in styling, but also in development process and in strategy.

Consumer-Centered Design:

We create consumer-focused experiences using branding, retail design, packaging, interaction (web) design & service design.

Design Skill Training:

We train both design professionals and non-design professional (business executives — managers, marketers & executive directors) on how to get the best results from design.

Service Enhancement:

We adopt design to help businesses review their existing offerings and where necessary, fine-tune their offerings to perfectly fit the needs of their customers.

Design Research:

We undertake design research assignments that provide organizations with the needed insight to create consumer-tailored products and services.

Why your business should consider adopting design...

01

Over the last 10 years design-led companies have maintained significant stock market advantage, outperforming the S&P by an extraordinary 211%

— Design Value Index (2016), Design Management Institute

02

Companies that work systematically with design have higher earnings and bigger exports than companies that do not use design.

— The Economic Effects of Design Report (2003), The National Agency for Enterprise and Housing of Denmark

03

On average, for every £1 businesses invest in design, they gain over £4 net operating profit, over £20 net turnover and over £5 net exports.

— The Design Council, UK
