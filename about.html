About Creadar

Originally established as network of creative entrepreneurs in 2014, we have evolved into an design-focused organization dedicated to creating value for entrepreneurs and enhancing business performance through the use of design.

Design doesn’t only impact the perception of a business; When adopted strategically, it creates value which stimulates innovation in a business.

— Our Belief

What we do

We are dedicated to helping businesses and organizations create lasting innovation through the use of design.

01

Startup Incubation

We use design as a key perspective in shaping startup companies into sustainable businesses. Our incubator strives at creating businesses that deeply integrate design in all they do.

02

Skill Training

We provide skill training on methodologies employed to attain satisfying results on the different roles design plays in business: design as strategy, as process and as styling.

03

Project Collaborations

Through partnerships, we share our expertise with cross-disciplinary social enterprises and nonprofits on projects focused on improving human and environmental wellbeing.

04

Design Support

We create consumer-focused experiences (branding, retail design, packaging, interaction design & service design) that enable organizations use design as a differentiator.

05

Design Awareness

To foster a deeper understanding of the strategic role design plays in business, we run campaigns demonstrating the value of design using in-house projects.

06

Service Enhancement

We adopt strategic design in guiding businesses to transform their services, enhance the efficiency of their supply chain and optimize their export strategies.

Partnerships

Our doors are open to institutions enable us, collaboratively, deliver all sorts of demands related to design.
Some institutions we engage through partnerships include:

— Private & public sector organizations
 — Small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs)
 — Nonprofits & Social Enterprises
 — Education Institutes & Research organizations
 — Designers, Business leaders & Academics

Governance

Creadar is the design advocacy wing of Creative Radar, a Ghanaian-registered nonprofit with its funding obtained from a combination of grants, partnerships, and paid-for assignments.

Our trustee board is composed of three units — Industry Associates, Review Committee & Partner Organizations , which work in sync to ensure the delivery of our brand promise: creating value for organizations and entrepreneurs using design.

Industry Associates

At Creadar, the voices of design experts we engage on our projects play a crucial role in our decision-making process. Their shared feedback at the end of each project informs our service design and helps us to continuously fine-tune our organization’s strategy. We therefore consider them as key drivers of our future.

Review Committee

We involve experts from cross-disciplinary areas to review the directions we come up with. Creadar is grateful to following experts for their insight in their respective areas:
Julian Cofie — Business Development, Mark Adjei — Research & Strategy, Dora Okyere — Healthcare, Kwaku Opuni — Non-profits & Social enterprises, and Gloria Akoto — Environmental Sustainability

Partner Organizations

The demands from our industry partners, both from objective-similar organizations and cross-disciplinary organizations, to some extent, shape our activities. We therefore welcome their opinions in our decision making process.

Each structure we put in place at Creadar is intended to help organizations reap design's value at its best: Strategic thinking, financial benefits, improved brand recognition, morale and productivity.

Nana Yaw Akuamoah
Strategy, Creadar

Creadar is the design advocacy program of Creative Radar, a Ghanaian-based nonprofit creating value for entrepreneurs through design-led innovations.
